# bpms-fights

100 health each  
bounce around arena  

## Teachers

---

- Mr. Miller  
  - Spawn sports equipment (footballs, basketballs, etc.) and orbit around him  
    - 4 damage per equipment  
    - 1.5 sec cooldown  
  - [PASSIVE] 25% damage deduction  

- Mr. Okamura  
  - Shoot feedback cards to opponents  
    - 5 damage  
    - Low accuracy  
    - 1 sec cooldown  

- Mr. Schatzlein  
  - Spawns a uranium ore where he is standing for 15 seconds  
    - Radius = 200 px: 0.2 damage every 0.5 sec
    - Radius = 175 px: 0.2 damage every 0.4 sec
    - Radius = 150 px: 0.2 damage every 0.35 sec
    - Radius = 100 px: 0.2 damage every 0.25 sec
    - Radius = 75 px: 0.2 damage every 0.15 sec
    - Radius = 50 px: 0.2 damage every 0.1 sec
    - 20 sec cooldown  
  - Calculates the distance and bounce physics of a rope with a ball attached for 3 sec, that bounces off walls(Max 10 times) until it hits the opponent.
    - (10 - bounces) damage
    - High accuracy
    - 6 sec cooldown 

- Mrs. Nguyen  
  - Shoots 3 cookies at opponents  
    - 3.5 damage  
    - 3 sec cooldown  
  - [PASSIVE] Every 13 seconds, eats a cookie to restore 15 health
